# 📊 Task 2: Phishing Simulation Results Interpretation (Mastercard Cybersecurity Program)

---

## 📌 Objective

Interpret phishing simulation results to identify vulnerable areas and design targeted security awareness training.

---

## 📊 Simulation Analysis

After conducting a phishing simulation campaign, user interactions were analyzed to evaluate security awareness across the organization.

---

## 🔍 Key Findings

- Some employees clicked on simulated phishing emails  
- A small percentage entered credentials on fake login pages  
- High-risk behavior observed in users unfamiliar with phishing indicators  
- Delayed reporting of suspicious emails in several cases  

---

## 🧠 Risk Areas Identified

- New employees with limited security awareness  
- Non-technical departments more likely to engage with phishing emails  
- Lack of awareness about verifying email authenticity  
- Weak recognition of suspicious links and urgency-based attacks  

---

## 🎓 Training Plan

To reduce risk and improve awareness, the following training measures are recommended:

- Regular phishing awareness workshops  
- Interactive phishing simulation exercises  
- Email verification best practices training  
- Real-world phishing case study demonstrations  
- Clear reporting procedure for suspicious emails  

---

## 🛡️ Outcome

The simulation highlights the importance of continuous security education. Targeted training significantly reduces the likelihood of successful phishing attacks and strengthens organizational cyber resilience.
